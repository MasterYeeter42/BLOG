Places You Must Visit

Efes BBQ

I visit on special occasions

Efes is a Turkish restaurant. I love the food, it is amazing. The food is always lovely and tasty. However, a bit on the pricier side but the portions are impossible to finish. The service is absolutely brilliant. I can't fault it. Overall, I liked the food, the general vibe and the wonderful staff. Would definitely go back there again! I highly recommend.
